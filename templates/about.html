About the Project
Welcome to my final project for CS50! My name is Dana Alnabulsi, and I created this game using an open Trivia API. With this website, you're able to register your unique account, and play a Trivia game that allows you to answer questions for points. You can customize these questions' category and difficulty, or allow for a mixed variety of the two. When the game is over, your game results are added to your games' history. Also, if you achieve a highscore, your highscore table is updated as well. Enjoy!
Inspiration Behind the Project
The inspiration came from my love for trivia games. I was in a club in highschool called Academic Games, a competition which has two teams answer random factual questions for points. I also loved learning the implementation of APIs during Finance, and thought it would be a good idea to explore that concept more during this project.
